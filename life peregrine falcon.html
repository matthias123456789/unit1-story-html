PEREGRINE FALCON'S LIFE

When the peregrine born, after 35-42 days with his/hers parents, becauese that days are perfect for learn how to fly. After he/she learned how to fly, the young peregnrine continue to rely on their parents for another 4-6 weeks for food and to master the flying skills they need in order to survive.

The places around the world where the peregrine falcon live

Peregnrine live almost everywhere on the planet. Their habitat can range from grasslands, deserts, artic tundras, and different types of forests. They can also live along the coast and around other bodies of water. Basically, peregrine can live just about anywhere they can find food.
